# Lineage 2 Interlude buffs and the classes that cast them.
# Format: [buff <name tokens>] One short true line about the buff or buffer class.

[buff prophet pp fighter] The Prophet (PP) is the human buffer; its kit includes Might, Shield, Wind Walk, Death Whisper, Guidance and Focus for fighters.
[buff prophet might shield] Prophet buffs like Might and Shield raise physical attack and defense for melee parties.
[buff elder ee elven mage] The Elven Elder (EE) buffs mages and supports with heals, Recharge (Mana regen) and Empower / Acumen style support.
[buff elder recharge mana] The Elven Elder can Recharge a party member's MP, which is vital for mages and other elders.
[buff shillien elder se dark elf] The Shillien Elder (SE) is the dark elf support, known for Death Whisper, Vampiric Rage and resists.
[buff warcryer wc orc] The Warcryer (WC) is the orc buffer, casting Chant buffs like Chant of Victory, Fury and Battle Heal.
[buff overlord op orc] The Overlord (OL) is an orc support caster with clan-style buffs.
[buff bishop heal mage cleric] The Bishop is the main healer, with strong heals, resurrection and a few utility buffs.
[buff song dance sws bd] Sword Singers (SwS) cast Songs and Blade Dancers (BD) cast Dances that buff a party's offense and defense.
[buff dance fury vampiric] Blade Dancer dances such as Dance of Fury and Dance of the Vampire boost attack speed and drain.
[buff song wind warrior] Sword Singer songs such as Song of Wind Storm and Song of Warding buff speed and defense.
[buff common might shield wind walk] Core party buffs most groups want are Might, Shield, Wind Walk, Death Whisper, Focus and Guidance.
[buff dwarf no caster] Dwarves (Warsmith / Bounty Hunter) are crafters and spoilers, not buffers.
