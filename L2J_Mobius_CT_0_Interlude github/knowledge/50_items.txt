# Lineage 2 Interlude common item shorthand and consumables.
# Format: [item <tokens>] One short true line mapping slang to the real item.

[item ss ssd ssc ssb ssa soulshot d c b a s] "ss" means Soulshot; "ssd" is Soulshot D-grade, "ssc" is Soulshot C-grade, and so on by grade.
[item bss bssd bssc bssb blessed soulshot] "bss" / "bssd" means Blessed Soulshot of that grade, a stronger soulshot.
[item sps spsd spsc spsb spiritshot d c] "sps" / "spsd" means Spiritshot D-grade; spiritshots power magic attacks.
[item bsps bspsd bspsc blessed spiritshot] "bsps" means Blessed Spiritshot, the stronger spiritshot for mages.
[item soe scroll escape] "soe" is a Scroll of Escape, which teleports you back to the nearest town.
[item sls scroll resurrection res] A resurrection scroll or a cleric's res restores most XP lost on death.
[item ewd ewc enchant weapon] "ewd" is Enchant Weapon D-grade; "ewc" Enchant Weapon C-grade; they upgrade weapons but can fail.
[item ead eac enchant armor] "ead" is Enchant Armor D-grade; "eac" Enchant Armor C-grade.
[item gemstone d c b] Gemstones (Gemstone D/C/B) are crafting and enchant materials.
[item iron ore mat material] Iron Ore, Coal, Charcoal and Animal Bone are common low grade crafting materials.
[item cp potion hp mp] HP potions heal health; "cp" potions restore CP; mana potions are limited in Interlude, so mages mostly rest.
[item adena price wts wtb] "WTS" means want to sell, "WTB" want to buy; prices are quoted in adena, often "k" (thousand) or "kk"/"m" (million).
[item d c b a s grade] Item grades go no-grade, D, C, B, A, S; quote the grade when buying or selling gear and shots.
