# Lineage 2 Interlude classes and roles.
# Format: [role <name tokens>] One short true line about the class or role.

[role human fighter knight warrior] Humans have strong knights (Paladin, Dark Avenger), warriors (Warlord, Gladiator) and the rogue Treasure Hunter.
[role human mystic wizard cleric] Human mystics become Sorcerer, Necromancer or Warlock (summoner), and the cleric line becomes Bishop or Prophet.
[role elf fighter knight scout] Elven fighters become Temple Knight, Sword Singer or the Plains Walker / Silver Ranger archer.
[role elf mystic elder spellsinger] Elven mystics become Spellsinger, Elemental Summoner or the support Elven Elder.
[role dark elf fighter assassin] Dark Elf fighters become Shillien Knight, Blade Dancer, Abyss Walker (dagger) or Phantom Ranger (archer).
[role dark elf mystic warlock se] Dark Elf mystics become Spellhowler, Phantom Summoner or the support Shillien Elder.
[role orc fighter destroyer tyrant] Orc fighters become the heavy Destroyer or the fist-fighting Tyrant.
[role orc mystic warcryer overlord] Orc mystics become the Warcryer or Overlord support casters.
[role dwarf crafter spoiler] Dwarves become the crafting Warsmith (Bounty Hunter line is the Scavenger / spoiler) and focus on making and gathering, not combat support.
[role tank aggro defense] Tanks (Paladin, Dark Avenger, Temple Knight, Shillien Knight) hold aggro and protect the party with high defense.
[role dagger backstab crit] Daggers (Treasure Hunter, Abyss Walker, Plains Walker) deal big critical/backstab damage from stealth.
[role archer bow ranged] Archers (Hawkeye, Silver Ranger, Phantom Ranger) deal ranged physical damage and kite mobs.
[role nuker mage sorcerer] Nukers (Sorcerer, Spellsinger, Spellhowler, Necromancer) deal heavy magic damage but need MP and shots.
[role healer bishop support] Healers (Bishop) keep the party alive with heals and resurrection; Elders also heal and recharge.
[role summoner pet] Summoners (Warlock, Elemental Summoner, Phantom Summoner) fight with a summoned servitor.
